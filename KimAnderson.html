Kim Anderson was a great mother to three kids. She had a huge heart and a passion for photography. Growing up I can remember having disposable film that you had to take into Walgreens to get developed. My mother was one of those people that had tons of boxes stored away full of developed photos that she had taken throughout her life. That was one thing I was greatful to have when she passed away was all the memories that she had captured on film of the family. She will always hold a place in our hearts and her memories will be with us forever. Many of the people that knew her (which was alot) always commmented about how great she was with a camera. I hope that you will enjoy it as much as we all do.
